      Two data layers. Ratings (radar, probabilities, xGe) come from real 2026+2025 results via shrinkage — always live. Tactical diagnostics (formation, 1v1, set-piece, aerial, press, finishing vs xG) come from the SCOUT object, which mirrors FBref's USL Championship schema (comp 73).       ▸ data schema & how to connect FBref
      SCOUT object — per team, mirrors FBref columns:
      poss possession % · sh90 shots/90 · sot SoT% · gsh G/Sh · npxg_diff np goals−xG (finishing) · tklpct tackle win% · past90 dribbled-past/90 · aerial aerial win% · setpiece_ga set-piece goals conceded · cross_against crosses faced/90 · err90 errors-to-shot/90 · hi_press att-third pressures · wing_ga_bias share of threat from wide · late_ga_share share of goals conceded after 70' · formation (Sofascore/FotMob).

      Connect it: FBref → fbref.com/en/comps/73/ (Standard, Shooting, Defense, Misc tables — free, Opta/StatsBomb). Formation → Sofascore or FotMob team page. Drop values into SCOUT[team], set DATA_MODE="live". Every threshold is a league-relative percentile, so flags self-calibrate to whatever data you load — no re-tuning.

      Diagnostic thresholds: defensive flag fires at ≥60th percentile on a bad-trait (e.g. dribbled-past), offensive flag at ≤35th on a good-trait (e.g. shot volume), strength at ≥65th. Finishing flags use raw np:G−xG (≤−2 wasteful, ≥+2.5 clinical).
